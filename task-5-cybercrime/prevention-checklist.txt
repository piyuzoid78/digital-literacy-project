Stay Safe Online Checklist:

* Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
* Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
* Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
* Always verify the sender of emails and messages
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
* Never share your OTP or banking details with anyone
* Check website URLs before entering personal information
* Install antivirus software and keep it updated

Report Cybercrime:

* Website: cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline: 1930
