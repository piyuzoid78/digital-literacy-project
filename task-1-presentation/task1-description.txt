I created a digital literacy infographic using Canva. The design highlights the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools, safe internet practices, and the importance of maintaining a professional online presence. I chose Canva because it is easy to use and offers a variety of templates that help in designing visually appealing content. One interesting part of this task was organizing information in a clear and attractive way. The most challenging part was selecting the most relevant points and keeping the design simple while making it informative.
